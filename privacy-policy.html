Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 1, 2025

Sudoku 25 respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our iOS app.

1. Information We Collect

Sudoku 25 does not collect any personally identifiable information. Your puzzle progress, game stats, and preferences are stored locally on your device.

2. Analytics

We do not use third-party analytics tools. All app activity remains private on your device.

3. Data Storage

We may use Apple’s iCloud or local storage to store your puzzle history and game preferences, depending on your iOS settings. No data is transmitted to our servers.

4. Email Subscriptions

If you choose to subscribe to updates (such as for multiplayer features), your email will be stored securely and used only to notify you. You can opt out anytime.

5. Contact Us

If you have questions about this policy, contact us at support@sudoku25.app.

This policy may be updated from time to time. We’ll notify you of significant changes through the app or website.
